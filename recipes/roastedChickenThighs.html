Super Easy Roasted Chicken Thighs

Description

Roasted chicken thighs that hubby says "almost taste like fried!"

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoon adobo seasoning, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place a rack on a baking sheet.
  2. Place chicken, skin-side down, on the rack. Liberally sprinkle with adobo seasoning.
    Turch chicken over, brush olive oil on skin, and liberally sprinkle with adobo.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until the jucies run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F(74 degrees C).
